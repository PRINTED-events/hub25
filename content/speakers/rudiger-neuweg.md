**Rüdiger Neuweg** is an internationally recognized expert in safety engineering and a pioneer in merging traditional engineering with additive manufacturing. Known in the global maker community as **@Groomakes**, he brings the precision and discipline of a seasoned safety engineer to the world of 3D printing, transforming technical complexity into innovative, practical solutions.

As a **Diplom-Ingenieur** in Safety Engineering and Managing Director of **Neuweg GmbH** since 2004, Rüdiger draws on over two decades of experience in quality management, fire protection, and laser safety. Through his ventures **NAS** and **NFS**, he demonstrates how 3D printing can fundamentally revolutionize professional training, safety communication, and operational workflows in industrial environments.

A prominent voice in the maker scene, he holds a key role in community-driven hardware development as an official beta tester and **Snapmaker Starmaker**. From the halls of **Formnext** to the stage of **Printed Amsterdam 2025**, Rüdiger shares his vision of how digital fabrication bridges the gap between corporate expertise and hands-on innovation. He represents a new generation of industrial makers—proving that 3D printing is not just a hobby, but a strategic tool for professionals redefining the future of their industries.
